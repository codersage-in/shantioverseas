ABOUT US

The company

The company has established its hold on the trade of Beans, Pulses, Lentils, Spices, Oilseeds, Rice, Animal Feed, Sugar, and Edible Nuts.

We have grown to handle a variety of commodities across continents.

Clientele Base

  1. Asia

    1. We procure beans, pulses, spices, nigerseed, sugar, rice from Myanmar, Thailand, Indonesia & supply to Indian sub continent, USA, Canada & Europe.
    2. We sell soybeans, soymeal, corn into Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand & Myanmar.

  2. North America

    1. We procure soybeans, soymeal, DDGS, Almonds from USA for sales into South East Asia.
    2. We procure Lentils and Yellow Peas from Canada for sales into Indian sub continent.
    3. USA & Canadian Buyers buy pepper, processed dals & spices from us for distribution. Our buyers are in New York, Houston, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Vancouver, Toronto, Oakland, Seattle and Chicago etc.

  3. South America

    1. Soybeans, soymeal is procured from Argentina, Uruguay, Paraguay for sales in asian & South East Asia Markets.

  4. Africa

    1. Edible nuts like Raw Cashewnuts are procured from various parts of Africa to meet the demand of buyers in India & Vietnam.
    2. We source pulses like Green Mung Beans, Pigeon Peas, Kidney Beans, Cowpeas for India, Indonesia, Malaysia.

  5. Europe

    We export “Nigar seed” to ports like Rotterdam and Felixstowe.
